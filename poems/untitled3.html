Written: 22/07/2022
Revised: 01/06/2025
Theme: quiet heartbreak, love and loss, unspoken pain, relationship grief

Looking into your eyes,
I no longer feel the same.
That joy and fullness I once felt,
Now only feels like empty fulfilment.
Being with you and being without you,
I feel empty all the same.

Notes: Another poem from the past, my older pieces seem to circle mostly around heartbreak. Considering they were all characters and I hadn't been going through any romantic distress this is a bit strange lol
