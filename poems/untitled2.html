Written: 29/08/2022
Theme: quiet heartbreak, emotional betrayal, unspoken pain, relationship grief

The spark we once had,
Which warmed my very soul.
Has faded to embers,
Nothing left but that bitter loneliness.
You're just out of reach,
But that's alright.
Because from the start-| knew,
It wouldn't last.
For this love-was never enough,
For the three of us.
